The names of the Brown County cemeteries have been obtained from the book Ohio Cemeteries: 1803-2003 by The Ohio Genealogical Society. Since this book is under copyright protection, I have been granted permission to publish the information by The Ohio Genealogical Society.

This book is a valuable resource for Ohio research. It includes cemetery directions, cemetery conditions, and other information. It may be purchased from The Ohio Genealogical Society.

If you have any information you would like to share about the cemeteries (condition, location, etc.), please submit them to the OGS Cemetery Committee, the Ohio Genealogical Society, 611 State Route 97 West, Bellville, OH 44813 or by e-mail to ogs@ogs.org. Please put "Cemeteries" in the subject area.
